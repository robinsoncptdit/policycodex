The Diocese of Pensacola-Tallahassee shall provide written acknowledgment to any donor making a contribution of $250 or more in a single transaction, in accordance with IRS Publication 1771. The acknowledgment shall state the amount of the contribution, describe any non-cash contributions, and confirm whether any goods or services were provided in exchange.

WHAT YOU ARE LOOKING AT

Base CSS: Bootstrap 5.3 from CDN. Every nav, button, table, form control, badge, progress bar, card, spinner, and collapse above uses Bootstrap classes.

Overlay: The 30-line block at the top of this file. Brand color comes from --bs-primary and a few button variable overrides. AI pills, gap badges, and the AI summary card are the only custom components.

HTMX: Loaded as a single <script> tag. Used only on screen 3 (file upload triggers extraction, panel swaps in place) and screen 4 (Add retention rule appends a row).

Build chain: none. CSS variable overrides handle theming.

Template cost: noticeably higher than the Pico variant. Every form control needs form-control, every button needs btn btn-primary, every nav needs the navbar class incantation. That's the trade.
