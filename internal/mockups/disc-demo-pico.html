The Diocese of Pensacola-Tallahassee shall provide written acknowledgment to any donor making a contribution of $250 or more in a single transaction, in accordance with IRS Publication 1771. The acknowledgment shall state the amount of the contribution, describe any non-cash contributions, and confirm whether any goods or services were provided in exchange.

WHAT YOU ARE LOOKING AT

Base CSS: Pico v2 classless. Every form, table, button, nav, hgroup, progress, details, dl, fieldset above is styled by Pico with no class needed.

Overlay: The 45-line block at the top of this file. Brand color comes from one variable. Gate badges, AI pills, gap badges, and the AI summary card are the only custom components.

HTMX: Loaded as a single <script> tag. Used only on screen 3 (file upload triggers extraction, panel swaps in place) and screen 4 (Add retention rule appends a row). aria-busy="true" handles the loading state.

Build chain: none. CSS variables are theme knobs.
