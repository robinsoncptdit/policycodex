## AI Integration Architecture

**Multi-LLM Strategy:**

- **Claude**: Policy clarity analysis, ethical implications review, nuanced language suggestions
- **GPT-4**: Compliance mapping, legal citation checking, technical accuracy
- **Gemini**: Cross-policy consistency, multilingual translation, data extraction
- **Specialized Models**:
    - Llama for on-premise sensitive policy analysis
    - Anthropic Constitutional AI for mission alignment scoring

**API Orchestration:**

- Central AI coordinator assigns tasks to appropriate LLMs based on strengths
- Parallel processing for time-sensitive reviews
- Consensus mechanism weighs outputs from multiple models
- Human-in-the-loop override always available

**Creative AI Applications:**

- **Policy DNA Fingerprinting**: AI creates unique signatures for each policy to track evolution
- **Sentiment Analysis**: AI monitors stakeholder feedback tone during review
- **Predictive Modeling**: AI forecasts which policies will need updates based on external regulatory changes
- **Auto-Documentation**: AI maintains real-time documentation of all policy decisions and rationales
